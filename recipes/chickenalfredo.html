Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a classic favorite among many. Its tasty, rich, creamy white sauce paired with thick strands of pasta and extremely tender, well-cooked chicken and tasty broccoli provides a great source of protein and satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of uncooked Fettuccine
  • 2 cups of fresh broccoli
  • 1/4 cup of KRAFT Zesty Italian Dressing
  • 1 pound of boneless an skinless chicken breasts cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 2/3 cups of Milk
  • 4 ounces of Philadelphia cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup of KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried basil leaves

Steps

  1. Cook pasta as directed on the package, and add broccoli to the boiling water for the last 2 minutes.
  2. Drain the pasta mixture
  3. Heat the dressing in a large, nonstick skillet on medium-high heat.
  4. Add the chicken and cook for 5 minutes, or until it's cooked through. Stir occasionally.
  5. Stir in milk, cream cheese, Parmesan cheese and basil. Bring to boil, stirring constantly.
  6. Cook until the sauce is well-blended and heated through.
  7. Add chicken mixture to the pasta mixture and mix it lightly.
