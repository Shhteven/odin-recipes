Salmon

Description

Salmon is one of the tastiest, most protein packed fish you could ever eat. It has a unique taste especially when prepared over charcoal or open flame. It develops a rich, buttery flavor that even seafood haters will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of Salmon fillets
  • Lemon pepper to taste
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • 1/3 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic poweder and salt.
  2. Stir soy sauce, brown sugar, water and vegetable oil together in a small bowl until sugar is dissolved.
  3. Place the salmon in a large resealable bag.
  4. Add soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat then refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  5. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat an lightly oil the grate.
  6. Place salmon on the preheated grill and discard marinade.
  7. Cook the salmon until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 6 to 8 minutes per side.
