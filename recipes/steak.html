Steak

Steak is one of the most popular foods to eat in BBQs. This thick cut of meat can be grilled or fried with rich, salty and smoky flavors and firm textures.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pound of flank steak

    • Marinade

  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1/3 cup of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons Worecestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk together oil, soy sauce, vinegar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic and pepper for marinade until thoroughly combined.
  2. Place the steak in a 9x13-inch glass baking dish.
  3. Pour the marinade over the flank steak in the baking dish. Turn several times to coat thoroughly with marinade.
  4. Cover and refrigerate it for 2-6 hours
  5. When ready to cook, preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  6. Remove the steak from the marinade and shake off the excess.
  7. Cook the steak on the preheated grill for about 5 minutes per side
  8. Remove from the grill and let it rest for about 5 minutes before slicing and serving
  9. Serve and Enjoy!
